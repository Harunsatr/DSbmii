Jobdesk Distribution

Team Leader

The team leader oversees project management and ensures effective communication among team members. They provide guidance, resolve conflicts, and ensure alignment with project objectives.

Front-end Developer

Front-end developers focus on creating visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. They collaborate closely with designers to bring design concepts to life and enhance user experience.

Back-end Developer

Back-end developers build the core functionality of the BMI classification project, ensuring scalability, security, and performance. They work with server-side logic and database management systems to deliver a robust solution.

Data Analyst

Data analysts extract insights from datasets to identify patterns and correlations related to body mass index. They clean and preprocess data, integrating their findings into the application to provide personalized recommendations for users.
